CONTROLS CRAFTING VILLAGERS HOMEPAGE TABLES
Enchantments are a way to improve the functionality and power up your tools and armor, this page will explain how to get enchantments and the beast enchantments are for most items in most situations. When dealing with enchantments the most common way of getting them is via an enchantment table which is made using 4 obsidian, 2 diamonds, and 1 book. The best way to use an enchanting table would be to surround the table in bookshelves like the image demonstrates, although this step is not necessary for you to enchant it allows you to obtain better enchantments. For enchanting you will need exp and lapis, once you have everything you need and insert your lapis and
your desired tool into the enchantment table you will seem three options that will light up depending on the amount of levels you have, the better enchants will be located at the bottom and will cost more levels. The options for enchantments will only change once you enchant an item, meaning that if you are looking for one specific enchantment and it doesnâ€™t show you will need to enchant something for the options to change. When you enchant an item with a for example 30 exp enchantment the table won't take all 30 levels only a small amount, and sometimes the enchantment table will give the tool a random enchantment. When using the enchantment table you can only enchant unenchanted items so if you are thinking of having multiple enchantments on one tool you should use an anvil. You can disenchant any item with a grindstone.
An anvil is also a tool used for enchanting, you can use an anvil for enchanting in many ways, one way is to combine two of the same type of item for the enchantments to transfer over. Another way of enchanting using an anvil would be to combine you item and an enchanted book.
