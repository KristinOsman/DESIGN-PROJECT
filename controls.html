HOMEPAGE CRAFTING VILLAGERS ENCHANTMENTS TABLES
These are some of the most important default contorls:
You can always change these controls by presing the ESCAPE BUTTON and OPTIONS, here you can also find the role of every key.
MOUSE MOUVEMENT (VERTICAL) Look up and down.
MOUSE MOUVEMENT (HORIZONTAL) Look left and Right
LEFT CLICK Pick an item up from a slot; split a stack evenly (hold and drag); sort loose items into one stack (double-click) Attack.
RIGHT CLICK Pick an item up from a slot; Pick an item on a slot in half; Place one item from a stack in each inventory slot (hold and drag).
WASD Keys cthat control where you move.
Q Key that drops items.
SHIFT Crouch
NUMUBER KEYS Correlate to the inventory slots.
CONTROL Sprint
E Open/Close inventory
ESCAPE KEY Opens up the menu and gives back cursor control. Pauses Singleplayer games. Closes any open GUI. Exits the chat/command window.
T and / Opens chat
SPACEBAR Jump
F Swap item to off hand.
